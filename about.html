Welcome to Journey Village Church

Journey Village Church was born out of a burning vision to see people encounter Christ and step into the life He gives—real and eternal life that restores what is broken, heals what is wounded, and overflows with more and better life than we ever dreamed. Our calling is to invite people to live the way of Christ through encounter, formation, community, and mission.

In November 2019, the vision took its first step forward, when Pastors Nash and Muthoni were commissioned by International Christian Centre Nairobi (ICCN) to plant a church in Kikuyu as part of the Kenya Assemblies of God (KAG). Leaving behind the familiar rhythms of ministry at JourneyFamily Church in Garden Estate, they stepped into the adventure of planting a new community of faith in the beautiful town of Kikuyu.

Early in 2020, a small core team began to gather, but soon the Covid-19 pandemic shutdown interrupted plans and scattered expectations. What could have ended the story became, instead, the soil for resilience and creativity.

Determined not to lose momentum, the team launched a weekly Village Gathering on Zoom. Though physically apart, God knit together hearts and laid the foundation for a worshipping community that longed for more.

When in-person gatherings were finally allowed, God provided a meeting place at Wattle House in Bethsaida Park. For three years, Wattle House became a sacred home—where a new body of Christ was launched, worship was lifted, friendships were forged, and faith was nurtured.

Yet the story was still unfolding. In 2023, God led us into a new chapter as the church leased land along Kikuyu–Kamangu Road. With prayer and sacrifice, what was once a thick forest was gradually reimagined into a gathering place for worship. In January 2024, the Journey Village family walked into its new home—a sign that God delights in transforming wilderness into sanctuaries of His presence.

Today, we continue to live into that founding vision. Journey Village is a community of faith, a people learning to live the way of Christ, as we encounter the risen Christ, be formed in His way, becoming His body for the sake of the world around us. Our story is still being written, and we believe the best is yet to come.

What Shapes and Guides Us

At Journey Village Church, we exist to invite people to live the way of Christ—through Christ-Encounter, Formation, Community, and Mission.

Our anchor Scripture is John 10:10: "A thief is only there to steal and kill and destroy. I came so they can have real and eternal life, more and better life than they ever dreamed of" (The Message). We long to be vessels of this promise, embodying and sharing the abundant life that Christ Himself offers.

1

Movement Upward (Worship)

We are anchored in the truth that we were created for worship—to love the Lord with all our heart, soul, mind, and strength. Our greatest joy is found in glorifying Him and delighting in His presence. This is our call to worship, the very life we were made for.

2

Movement Inward (Wholeness)

We believe that Christ invites us into a journey of salvation, healing, and restoration—a transformation that touches every part of our being. As His life takes root within us, we are shaped by the Spirit and begin to bear His fruit.

3

Movement Withward (Community)

We embrace the truth that faith is lived in community, for none of us are the body of Christ alone. We grow as we give and receive grace, share burdens, and celebrate joys. In community, our gifts take shape, our love becomes visible, and together we become a living witness of Christ's presence.

4

Movement Downward (Service)

At the end of His ministry, Jesus washed His disciples' feet and called us to the same way of love. For us at the Village, we believe that true greatness is found in humility, as we follow Christ who came not to be served but to serve.

5

Movement Outward (Missional)

We believe that as followers of Jesus, we are a sent people—called to the poor, to make disciples, and to be salt and light in the world. Faith is never private; it overflows into our neighborhoods, workplaces, and even to the ends of the earth.

6

Movement Backward (Rooted)

We stand in the truth that to know where we're going, we must first look back at where we've been. God's story of grace—from creation to Christ to the promised new heaven and new earth—now includes us, and we root ourselves in His Word to be shaped as His people.

7

Movement Forward (Journey)

We affirm with conviction that God has ordained a purpose for each of us today, and as we live it out, we join His work of shaping the future He desires. We are not called merely to exist but to leave fingerprints of faith, hope, and love where we are planted.

Our Leadership

Pastor Nash Waruguru

Lead Pastor

After graduating from University of Nairobi with a degree in Economics and Political science, Nash joined The Journey, a campus ministry of ICC Nairobi. He served in full time ministry since 2008, investing in students across 4 universities.

In 2019, he transitioned from college ministry to planting The Journey Village Church alongside his wife Muthoni.

Nash and Muthoni are blessed with three wonderful children.

Mũthoni Maina-Mwangi

Sound of the Village Coordinator

The Sound of the Village is our musical worship team, led by Muthoni, who is passionate about helping people encounter Jesus through musical worship. She leads and serves the team as they guide the church in worship week after week. Muthoni is especially excited about raising up a younger generation of worshippers at The Village, equipping them to grow in both their musical gifts and their walk with Christ.

Wambũi Mũrĩmi

Village Kids Coordinator

Wambũi leads the team that serves and cares for our kids each week. With a deep passion for children, she is committed to creating spaces where they encounter God's love, are taught to love one another in community, and are empowered to grow into the fullness of who He created them to be.

Anne Mwanĩkĩ

Administrator

Anne serves the Village by ensuring we steward well all that God entrusts to us. She oversees our finances, programs, events, and logistics with diligence and care, helping the church function smoothly and effectively. Her deep passion is to see God's Kingdom built through wise, faithful and accountable stewardship of His resources.
