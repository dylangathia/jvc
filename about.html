Our Story & Mission

Discover the heart behind Journey Village Church and our commitment to serving the Kikuyu community

Welcome to Journey Village Church

Journey Village Church was founded in 2020 with a vision to create a Christ-centered community where people can find belonging, grow in faith, and serve others with their God-given gifts.

Located in the heart of Kikuyu, we are a diverse community of believers committed to making a positive impact in our neighborhood and beyond. Our church family represents different ages, backgrounds, and walks of life, united by our love for Jesus Christ.

We believe that church is not just a building or a Sunday event, but a family on mission together. Whether you're new to faith or have been following Jesus for years, there's a place for you here at Journey Village.

Our Core Values

Authentic Community

We prioritize genuine relationships where people can be real, find support, and grow together in faith.

Biblical Truth

We are committed to the authority of Scripture and teaching that transforms lives.

Intentional Discipleship

We help people become fully devoted followers of Christ through mentoring and spiritual growth opportunities.

Compassionate Service

We demonstrate God's love by serving our community and meeting practical needs.

What We Believe

Our Statement of Faith

We believe in one God who exists in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God who died for our sins and rose again, offering salvation to all who believe.

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and our ultimate authority for faith and practice. We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit to transform lives and empower believers for service.

We believe the Church is the body of Christ on earth, called to worship God, make disciples, and serve the world in love. We believe in the personal return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of all people to eternal life or judgment.
