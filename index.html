Life At The Village

Encounter Sunday

Every Sunday we gather to worship Christ—seeking a fresh encounter with Him, being shaped into His likeness, and growing together as a community of faith. From this place of worship, we are sent out into the world to join God's mission with love and courage.

Village Kids

Our Village Kids Ministry serves children of all ages, not merely through programs, but through relationships that nurture faith and point them to Christ. We focus on sharing the story of God's love so that every child may encounter Christ personally, grow in His likeness, and live for His purposes in the world.

Village Bomas

Each month, neighbours in our community gather to remember that life is best lived in circles. To live the way of Christ is to step out of isolation and into belonging, knowing that faith is never a solo project but a shared journey. In our Bomas we check in on one another, celebrate together, and carry each other's burdens.

Ministry At The Village

Birthday Sunday

Every month we joyfully celebrate and bless those with birthdays, thanking God for the gift of their lives. It is more than just a moment of cake and fellowship—it is an opportunity to honor Christ's work in each person, to speak words of blessing, and to rejoice together as one family in Him.

Ladies' and Men's Hangouts

We believe following Christ shapes every part of life, including our callings as women and men. Our hangouts provide space for honest conversations, shared wisdom, and encouragement as we grow in Christ, strengthen one another in faith, and live out our God-given identity for His kingdom.

Parenting Ministry

Parenting today is both a challenge and a sacred calling. In the midst of busy lives, we long to raise children who know and follow Jesus. Our Parenting Ministry walks alongside all parents—biological or spiritual—equipping and encouraging them to pass on faith, wisdom, and love that form the next generation in Christ.
